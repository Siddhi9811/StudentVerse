Master of Technology (MTech)

The Master of Technology (MTech) is a two-year postgraduate program that provides advanced knowledge and skills in various engineering and technology fields. The program offers specializations such as Computer Science, VLSI Design, Software Engineering, and more.

Curriculum

Semester Subjects
Semester 1 Advanced Engineering Mathematics, Research Methodology, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, Data Structures
Semester 2 Machine Learning, Database Management Systems, Operating Systems, Digital Signal Processing, Elective I
Semester 3 Cloud Computing, Mobile Application Development, Cyber Security, Elective II, Project Work (Part I)
Semester 4 Big Data Analytics, Internet of Things, Major Project, Industrial Training
