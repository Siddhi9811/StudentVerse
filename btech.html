Bachelor of Technology (BTech)

The Bachelor of Technology (BTech) is a four-year undergraduate program in engineering that covers the fundamentals of technology, science, mathematics, and engineering principles. The program offers specializations in various fields such as Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and more.

Curriculum

Semester Subjects
Semester 1 Engineering Mathematics I, Physics for Engineers, Engineering Chemistry, Introduction to Programming, Basic Electrical Engineering
Semester 2 Engineering Mathematics II, Mechanics of Solids, Engineering Graphics, Digital Logic Design, Computer Programming
Semester 3 Engineering Mathematics III, Electrical Circuits, Signals and Systems, Thermodynamics, Materials Science
Semester 4 Engineering Mathematics IV, Fluid Mechanics, Data Structures and Algorithms, Electronics, Mechanics of Materials
Semester 5 Control Systems, Microprocessors and Microcontrollers, Electromagnetic Fields, Structural Analysis, Management and Entrepreneurship
Semester 6 Communication Systems, Power Systems, Manufacturing Processes, Software Engineering, Environmental Engineering
Semester 7 VLSI Design, Power Electronics, Database Management Systems, Electrical Machines, Project Work (Part I)
Semester 8 Machine Learning, Renewable Energy, Engineering Ethics, Major Project, Industrial Training
