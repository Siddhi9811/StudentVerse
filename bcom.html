Bachelor of Commerce (BCom)

The Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) is a three-year undergraduate degree course in the field of commerce. This program is designed to provide a foundational understanding of commerce-related subjects such as accounting, economics, finance, and business law.

Curriculum

Semester Subjects
Semester 1 Financial Accounting, Business Mathematics, Business Economics, English Communication, Introduction to Computers
Semester 2 Corporate Accounting, Microeconomics, Business Organization, Business Statistics, Environmental Studies
Semester 3 Cost Accounting, Macroeconomics, Business Law, Marketing Management, Financial Management
Semester 4 Management Accounting, International Business, Taxation, Human Resource Management, Entrepreneurship Development
Semester 5 Financial Markets, Business Research Methods, Corporate Finance, Auditing, Income Tax Law and Practice
Semester 6 Advanced Accounting, Indirect Taxes, Business Ethics, Strategic Management, Project Work
