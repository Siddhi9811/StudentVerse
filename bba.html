Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a three-year undergraduate program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of business principles, management practices, and entrepreneurship.

Curriculum

Semester Subjects
Semester 1 Principles of Management, Business Communication, Financial Accounting, Business Mathematics, Microeconomics
Semester 2 Organizational Behavior, Business Statistics, Marketing Management, Macroeconomics, Business Law
Semester 3 Human Resource Management, Financial Management, Production and Operations Management, Business Research Methods, Elective I
Semester 4 Strategic Management, International Business, Entrepreneurship Development, Elective II, Project Work (Part I)
Semester 5 Business Ethics, E-Commerce, Consumer Behavior, Elective III, Project Work (Part II)
Semester 6 Business Policy, Corporate Governance, Elective IV, Internship, Major Project
